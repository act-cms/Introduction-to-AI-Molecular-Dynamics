AI-Assisted MD Workflows
========================

This lesson introduces responsible use of AI and LLMs in molecular dynamics
workflows. Students will use AI tools to plan simulations, draft scripts,
summarize outputs, and accelerate analysis while validating every result.

## Lesson Goals

1. Use LLMs to draft and revise LAMMPS, Atomsk, and analysis scripts.
2. Audit AI-generated code for physical and technical correctness.
3. Summarize logs, outputs, and trajectory-analysis results.
4. Understand limitations of AI assistants and machine-learned MD models.

## Suggested Contents

* `student.ipynb`: Guided AI-assisted workflow exercises.
* `instructor-key.ipynb`: Completed examples with critique and corrections.
* `prompts/`: Example prompts and prompt-evaluation notes.
* `lammps/`: AI-drafted and corrected simulation scripts.
* `analysis/`: Python analysis scripts and notebooks.
* `figures/`: Workflow diagrams and validation plots.
